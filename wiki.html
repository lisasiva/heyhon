HeyHon

Organize the life you share in one place

HeyHon is a Notion template for people in relationships, where you can brain dump household tasks and important dates—so no one has to do all the remembering.

How it works

15 Minutes. 3 Thoughtful Prompts

Get your reflection

You'll get one email on Sunday with 3 new prompts to reflect on the past week, and the week to come.

Take 15 min to reflect

Hit the forward button and type right in the body of the email. Whatever's on your mind, you can offload it here.

Save it or send it

You can save the email as a draft, just for you to look back on—or send it on to your partner, so they know what's on your mind.

Why relationship reflections?

Lighten Your Mental Load

Get some headspace

There's so much we have to do to keep our lives up and running. Weekly reflections give you space to offload the stuff in your head, right into your inbox.

Do it solo. Share whenever

If it feels right, you can forward your reflection with one click. And because your partner already uses email, you know they'll actually see it.

Be intentional about life

Don't let your relationship go on autopilot. Each reflection is a reminder to slow down and make sure you and your relationship are always improving.
