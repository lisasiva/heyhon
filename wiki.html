HeyHon

Organize the life you share in one place

Think of HeyHon as your relationship wiki, a place where you can brain dump household tasks and important dates—so no one has to do all the remembering.

What you get

A knowledge base for your relationship

1. To-dos

Keep track of household tasks with a Trello-like board—so both of you know what's happening, and who's taking care of what.

2. Important Dates

See all your most important dates and milestones at a glance, as a calendar or as a timeline.

3. Ideas for Later

Save ideas for date night and movies to watch, so you don't end up endlessly scrolling through Netflix.

4. Weekly Check-ins

You'll get a template for weekly meetings, inspired by engineering retrospectives, to sync up on things like successes, to-dos, and challenges.

How it works

A template for Notion users

Sign up for Notion

If you don't already use Notion, it's easy (and free!) to sign up here.

Grab the template

Drop your email at the top or bottom of this page. You'll get a link to the template.

Share it with your partner

Poke around, and then share it with your partner, so you can both be on the same page.

Why HeyHon?

Share the Mental Load

Get some headspace

There's so much we have to do to keep our lives up and running. A relationship wiki gives you space to offload the stuff in your head, so you don't have to do all the remembering.

Make the work you do visible

A lot of what we do—the research, the thinking ahead—is usually invisible. By putting those tasks down in a shared place, we help our partners know what's on our minds.

Be intentional about life

Don't let your relationship go on autopilot. The weekly check-ins are reminders to slow down and make sure you and your relationship are always improving.
