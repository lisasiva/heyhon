HeyHon

Organize the life you share in one place

HeyHon is your relationship wiki, a centralized place where you can brain dump household tasks and important dates—so no one partner has to do all the remembering.

What you get

A knowledge base for your relationship

1. Weekly To-dos

Keep track of household tasks with a Trello-like board, so both of you know what's happening, and who's taking care of what.

2. Important Dates

See what's coming up, whether it's a trip, job interview, or important milestone, on one shared calendar.

3. Ideas for Later

Save your ideas for date nights, so that when the weekend rolls around, there's always something new to try.

4. Weekly Check-ins

Get on the same page with 4-question templates for weekly meetings, to help you sync up on all of the above.

How it works

5 minutes to get started

Grab the template

This template is built for Notion, a project management tool. If you already use Notion, just hit "duplicate." Otherwise, it's free to sign up here.

Add this week's to-dos

Start with the page called "Weekly To-Dos." All the stuff that needs to get done this week? Brain dump it here.

Share it with your partner

Feel free to move stuff around in the template until it feels nice and cozy. Then, when you're ready, hit Share > Invite a Person.

Why HeyHon?

Share the Mental Load

Get some headspace

There's so much we have to do to keep our lives up and running. A relationship wiki gives you space to offload the stuff in your head, so you don't have to do all the remembering.

Make the work you do visible

A lot of what we do—the research, the thinking ahead—is usually invisible. By putting those tasks down in a shared place, we help our partners know what's on our minds.

Be intentional about life

Don't let your relationship go on autopilot. The weekly check-ins are reminders to slow down and make sure you and your relationship are always improving.
