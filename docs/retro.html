What you get

Simple, Satisfying Relationship Retros

1. Four useful questions

On the Retro tab, you'll find four questions to help you celebrate wins, sync up on chores, plan date ideas, and problem-solve challenges.

2. Action items

As things come up—like a bill that needs paying—you can jot them down in your Action Items list. This way, both of you know what's happening, and who's taking care of it.

3. 30-second tips

Tap the Help tab for practical, bite-sized nuggets of wisdom on topics like: What to do during a retro, when and where to do it, and how to make sure you're in the right headspace.

How it works

30 Minutes. 4 Questions. Once a Week.

Pick a time

Schedule 30 minutes of face-to-face time. (Sunday evening after dinner is a popular choice.)

Open the app

Inside, you'll find 4 questions—plus space for action items and topics you'll hold off on discussing until next retro.

Discuss

Set a timer for 30 minutes. Then, discuss each question, syncing up on chores, date ideas, and goals.

Reconnect

After your retro, thank each other. Celebrate with snuggles, ice cream, or whatever you enjoy best!

Why retrospectives?

Get on the Same Page

Save conversations for later

Knowing you have a relationship retro coming up, you can comfortably put a pin in discussions that might otherwise throw off your day.

Lighten your mental load

When you get on the same page about chores and problems that need solving, no one person has to be responsible for remembering all the things.

Show up for each other

In the craziness of life, retros help you make time to support the person you care about most, and to make sure your relationship is always improving.
